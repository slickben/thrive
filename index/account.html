Accounting

product / Accounting

Manage Your Finances With Thrive Cloud Accounting Software

Thrivecloud's financial management solution expedites daily financial transactions, accelerates the financial close and ensures compliance. Our cloud-based platform ensures complete real-time visibility into the financial performance of the business from a consolidated level down to the individual transactions.

Accounting module seamlessly integrates with all Sale management, inventory, CRM and ecommerce functions to streamline critical business processes.

Real-Time View Of Your Accounting Books With Dashboards

Dashboards provide an at-a-glance view of key performance indicators relevant to the business processes. With provision to create configurable accounting dimensions and customizable dashboards, you can now analyze each component of your business in one place.

Chart of Accounts

Being a configurable tree view for structuring accounting ledgers as desired, the Chart of Accounts forms the blueprint of your company. Groups can have sub-groups and ledgers within them, whereas ledgers are the leaf nodes of your chart and cannot be further classified.

Billing And Pricing

Invoice your customers, Manage receivable payments with payment requests and send notifications via email/SMS reminders. Create custom print format templates for bills, and set up defaults. As for Item Pricing, it's a one-time configuration and auto-fetched in transactions.

Journals and Payments

Maintain a clean and crisp ledger of accounts to avoid mismatches when closing accounts. Track your income and expenses - be it deferred or accrued, set up notifications for period closing, control your cashflow. Advance payments can be recorded and reconciled in one go.

Reduce time spent on mundane tasks for products or services that require renewals after certain time periods. Use Subscriptions and get invoices generated automatically for subscription orders.

Banking

Create unlimited bank and cash accounts and track their opening and current balances. Maintain bank accounts for your suppliers and customers. Ensure that your financials are synchronized with one-click bank statement upload. Clearance made easy.

Customizations

Map important data specific to your business by adding custom fields in your forms. Customize form behavior by auto-fetching values, hide fields based on user roles, create custom Print Formats all without writing a single line of code.
