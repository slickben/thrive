Frequently Asked Questions

Whats Included In Our Offer

Thriveccloud Is A Service Provider, We Pride Ourselves To Provide A Platform Service To Manage Your Business In The Cloud By Leveraging On Erpnext, The World's Best Opensouce ERP. Below Are What We Cover In The Platform Subscription Service

- Hosting, Backups, Updates And Storage Managed By Us. - Priority Functional Support By The Thrive Cloud Team - Your Own Subdomain (With A Choice Of Accessing Via Your Custom Domain). - World Class Implementation Service (Subject To Additional Charges)

Is The Cost Of Implementation Covered In The Subscription Plan?

Our platform is designed for self-implementation, and most companies don't need any help, just a bit of determination. However we offer a dedicated implementation service to help most business get the best out of the platform and speed up the adoption process. Learn more about our implementation service

Can I Get A One-On-One Demo?

Yes, we would be happy to schedule a remote, one-on-one demo. Just send us a request for one.

Can I Pay Via Credit Card Or Wire Transfer?

Yes, you can. We accept payment for both local and international transfer via Flutterwave payment platform. This allows you to make payment from within your account using your credit card or your bank account. If you prefer to pay with wire transfer please contact our sales team.

Thrivecloud Leverages On Opensource Erp Software, Then Why Charge Per User?

ERPNext is Free and Open Source ("free" as in "free-speech"), but the services around ERPNext need engineers to support you. These are what we charged for and this is why we have created thrive cloud as a platform service to help business leverage the best of what Open source ERP Can Offer and make it available across all size of businesses. Since scale, complexity and support requirement of a deployment is proportional to the number of users, the Enterprise Support contract is also priced per user. Also most ERP products are priced per user, so it is easy to compare with other products. Thrive Support and platform service is a fraction of what you would pay for NetSuite, SAP or Oracle Cloud.
